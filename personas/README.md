# heyron® Agent Personas

**52 ready-to-use SOUL.md files.** Copy one, paste it as your agent's personality, and go. Free forever.

## How to Use

**Option 1: Replace your main agent's personality**
Tell your agent in chat:
> "Use this as your SOUL.md" and paste the contents of any persona file.

**Option 2: Create a sub-agent with a specialty**
Tell your agent:
> "Create a sub-agent called 'copywriter' and use the copywriter SOUL.md from ~/agent-library/personas/"

**Option 3: Mix and match**
Take sections from different personas and combine them into your own custom SOUL.md.
